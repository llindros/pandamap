About This Map

This map attempts to include every panda loaned or gifted outside of Mainland China since the beginning of "panda diplomacy" in the 1940s.

Click on a panda icon to see individual zoos and the pandas they have housed.

Each panda is represented on a timeline showing their birthday and any relevant loan events.
